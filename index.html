What's New

  • Children's Bathrooms and Canteen are Both Rennovated and Improved



    The cafeteria received better lunch tables, overhead guards to protect from rain, and water fountains, as well a mural on a wall. The children's bathrooms recieved a no-touch hand dryer, better sinks, a mirror, and two murals (one on the floor and one on the ceiling).

  • An Online Student Check-in-Check-out system has been implemented

    The school has added a student check-in-check-out system for security purposes. A student will check in at the start of the day, and will automatically be checked in to all the applications that are associated with the school's main database. This is the same when a student checks out at the end of the day. Students will check in to the homework database to finish homework assignments.There are to different codes for every student. One for Classwork and one for Homework.
